About Me

Ben

Hello! I currently work as a draftsmen/fabricator for a company in the Salt Lake Area. My wife and I have been married for nearly a year and we love to go to the movies (or our living room, COVID-19 style) and eat lots of popcorn.

Currently, I run the fabrication department of a truck and trailer shop and I am pursuing a B.S. in Applied Technology. I love the flexibility and Christ-centered focus of BYU-I. It has helped me to grow in my faith.

I am taking this course as part of my degree. The future of the economy is going to be increasingly web-based and I want to be on the cutting edge of that technology. Additionally, I hope that I can take the skills I learn here and apply them to my job in some way. I also would like to help my wife with increasing the online presence of her waxing business.

I have been a member of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints for my entire life. It has challenged me and helped to find peace in a challenging world. One of my favorite scriptures is 3 Nephi 27:27, when Christ says, "Therefore, what manner of men ought ye to be? Verily I say unto you, even as I am." I often come back to these words when I am reviewing my life and seeking to find a happier path.
